Who We Are

Above all, Preserve The Peace is a vibrant online community of people all around the world, dedicated to supporting each other's mental health journeys. Beyond our online community, we created a mental health support network, offering advantages such as open peer support groups and mental health workshops and programs in our local region in WW-P(West-Windsor and Plainsboro) open to all, at no cost.

How We Support Our Community

We understand that mental health isn't something you get to hear about everyday. That's why Preserve The Peace offers the following programs to support the members of our community, all totally free of charge in order to raise awareness and breakdown the modern stigma and ongoing challenge of mental health!
